Plugin Deliciousbadge MooTools

This plugin allows you to create a badge for your website that loads content from the social bookmarking site del.icio.us. The badge will be relatively unobtrusive to visitors, and when it's clicked, it will expand to display the site owner's most recently added bookmarks.

Syntax:

new Deliciousbadge(options);

My Latest Bookmarks 

new Deliciousbadge({badgeid: 'mydelicious',
                    outputid: 'output1',
                    amount: 10});
Your Latest Bookmarks 

new Deliciousbadge({badgeid: 'yourdelicious',
                    outputid: 'output2',
                    amount: 10});
